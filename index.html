Auto Agent
MVP Technical Specs v1.0

Vehicle Telematics & AI Assistant

Modernizing affordable cars with a Raspberry Pi-powered "Smart Brain," high-fidelity digital cockpit, and Gemini-driven diagnostics.

MVP Hardware Architecture

  • Core: Raspberry Pi 4/5 (Broadcom BCM2711/2712).
  • Vehicle Interface: USB OBD-II Adapter (ELM327 Chipset) for maximum stability.
  • Data Link: 4G/5G Cellular Modem for real-time cloud synchronization.
  • Location: USB GPS Module for tracking and geofencing.
Raspberry PiELM327LTE/5GGPS

Software Tech Stack

Backend & Processing
  • Language: Python (python-obd library).
  • Real-time Stream: Firebase Realtime Database (for 5Hz gauges).
  • Cold Storage: PostgreSQL (for health logs and driving scores).
  • AI: Gemini 1.5 Flash (Diagnostic translation & repair plans).

Digital Cockpit (UI/UX)

Focus: "Eyes on the Road"
  • Framework: Flutter for Web (60 FPS high-fidelity gauges).
  • Primary View: Dynamic radial Gauges (RPM/Speed) and "Contextual Leaf" for efficiency feedback.
  • Mechanic View: 3D Wireframe of car with color-coded health alerts.

Data Acquisition Strategy

Frequency Parameters (PIDs) Purpose
5 Hz (High) RPM, Throttle, Steering Angle Live Gauges / Safety Alerts
1 Hz (Mid) Coolant Temp, Fuel Level, Speed Efficiency & Health Monitoring
30s (Low) DTCs (Error Codes), Engine Load AI Diagnosis / MIL Alerts

Key MVP Features

AI Master Mechanic
  • Natural Language Fixes: Converts complex codes into clear DIY steps via Gemini.
  • Price Transparency: Direct comparison of DIY parts cost vs. Local Mechanic labor rates.
Safety & Monitoring
  • Parental Control: Real-time GPS tracking and geofence alerts.
  • Driving Scorecard: Smoothness-based grades to lower insurance premiums.
  • Collision Alarm: Emergency triggers based on accelerometer/RPM spikes.
