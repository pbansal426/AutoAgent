AutoAgent Project
Gen2 MVP Architecture

1. The Core Concept

AutoAgent is a smart bridge between your car and your phone. It takes messy data from your car’s engine (the Car End) and turns it into simple, helpful advice on your app (the User End) using an AI Master Mechanic and a team of intelligent agents.

2. System Segmentation

📡 The Car End IN DEVELOPMENT

The physical "Brain" of the operation acting as the primary data collector and real-time sentinel.

  • Interface: Plugs into the standard OBD2 port.
  • Processing: Raspberry Pi handles initial data filtering.
  • High-Speed Telemetry: Captures 5 Hz data (RPM, Throttle, Speed).
  • Persistence: Onboard battery and GPS for "Heartbeat" tracking while off.

📱 The User End

The interface where data becomes value. This is the primary portal for the driver.

  • The App: A React-based web and mobile dashboard.
  • The Vault: Secure PostgreSQL database for user profiles and vehicle history.
  • AI Hosting: Backend logic where Gemini and LangGraph process raw data into repair plans.

3. The AI Intelligence Stack

AutoAgent uses a specialized team of AI workers that share the same "brain" but have different jobs. This setup ensures the car is monitored from every angle.

Core Intelligence

  • Gemini 1.5 Flash: Raw AI power providing high-speed reasoning and code interpretation.
  • LangGraph: The digital manager ensuring agents follow logical rules and safety paths.

Specialized Nodes

  • SafetyNode: Detects crashes or mechanical failures instantly.
  • TripAnalystNode: Post-drive scans for driving scores and small glitches.
  • TrendStrategistNode: Weekly scans for long-term health (battery/MPG).

4. MVP Feature Set

  • Live Cockpit: Real-time engine performance via WebSockets.
  • AI Master Mechanic: On-demand P-code translation to plain English.
  • Safety Heuristics: Crash detection via G-force/Speed fluctuations.
  • Smart Garage: Cloud-based management for VIN and maintenance logs.
  • Gamified Efficiency: AI-generated Driver Scores to improve safety and fuel use.
